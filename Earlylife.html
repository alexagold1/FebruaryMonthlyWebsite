Early Life

Earhart is Born

Amelia Mary Earhart was born on July 24, 1897 in Atchison, Kansas. She spent most of her time with her grandparents up until they died. After, her family struggled financially as well with her fathers use of Alcohol.

Earhart Education

The Earharts moved often, and she completed high school in Chicago in 1916. Then Earhart was able to attend the Ogontz School in Rydal, Pennsylvania. However, Amelia developed an interest in caring for soldiers wounded in World War I. In 1918 she left junior college to become a nurse’s aide in Toronto.

Earhart Flies

Shortly after, she went on her first airplane ride in 1920, an experience that prompted her to take flying lessons. In 1921 she bought her first plane, a Kinner Airster, and two years later she earned her pilot’s license.
