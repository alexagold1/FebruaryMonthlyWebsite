Early Life & Education

Childhood

Joe Biden was born November 20th, 1942 in Scranton, Pennsylvania. He lived with his parents, Catherine Eugenia Finnegan, and Joseph R. Biden Sr as well as his siblings, James Biden, Francis W. Biden, and Valerie Biden Owens. Learn more!

Education

He attended University of Delaware in 1965 where he received his bachelor's degree. After completing his education at Delaware, he then attended Syracuse University where he received his law degree. Learn more!

Before entering Politics

After Graduating from Syracuse, he took a job with a corporate law firm defending big businesses. He then realized that it wasn't right for him and then became a public defender, primarily for African Americans. Learn more!
