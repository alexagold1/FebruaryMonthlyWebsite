Fight for Presidency

The basics

Apart of the Democratic Party and ran against the republican, Donald J Trump, who previously served a term in the white house.

What was he about?

His campaign platform was primarily about: Racial equity and equal opportunity, the military, Fixing problems in student loan programs, manufacturing, and Creating well paid jobs.

Results

Ultimately, he beat donald trump in the electoral college vote 306-232, and had a 4 point margin in the popular vote
