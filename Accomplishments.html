Joe Biden's Presidency Timeline

January 20, 2021

Inauguration

Joe Biden was inaugurated as the 46th President of the United States.

February 14, 2021

COVID-19 Relief Bill Passed

Biden signs a $1.9 trillion COVID-19 relief package into law to aid the economy and the fight against the pandemic.

August 15, 2021

Afghanistan Withdrawal

The United States completed its military withdrawal from Afghanistan, ending a 20-year conflict.

November 15, 2021

Infrastructure Bill Signed

Biden signed a $1 trillion infrastructure bill into law to rebuild roads, bridges, and public transit.

March 2022

Russia Invades Ukraine

Biden's administration imposed heavy sanctions on Russia in response to its invasion of Ukraine.

December 2022

Omicron Surge

The Omicron variant of COVID-19 caused a surge in cases, leading to renewed focus on testing and vaccination campaigns.

November 2024

Midterm Elections

The midterm elections saw shifts in congressional control, with the Democratic party maintaining a slim majority in the Senate.
